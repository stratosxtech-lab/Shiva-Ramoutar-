Your Data Deletion Rights

Under various privacy laws including GDPR (European Union), CCPA (California), and other applicable regulations, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. We respect this right and have established a clear process for handling deletion requests.

What Happens When You Request Deletion

When we receive and verify your data deletion request, we will process it according to the following guidelines:

Data We Will Delete:

  • Account Information: Your email address, user ID, and profile data
  • Subscription Records: Your subscription will be cancelled and billing information removed from our systems (Stripe may retain transaction records as required by law)
  • Usage Data: Your app usage history and preferences stored on our servers
  • Push Notification Tokens: Your device tokens for notifications
  • Support History: Any support tickets or communications (unless required for legal purposes)

Data Stored Locally (Your Responsibility):

  • Documents & Photos: Files stored in the Document Storage feature are on YOUR device only - uninstall the app to remove them
  • Emergency Contacts: Contact information stored locally on your device
  • Checklist Progress: Your preparedness checklist data
  • App Preferences: Settings and preferences stored on your device

Data We May Retain:

  • Transaction Records: Payment history may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Legal Obligations: Data required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you may be retained for analytics

Deletion Timeline

Step Timeline
Request Acknowledgment Within 48 hours
Identity Verification 1-3 business days
Data Deletion Completed Within 30 days of verification
Confirmation Email Upon completion

How to Submit a Deletion Request

Send an email to stratosxtech@gmail.com
Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
Include in your email:
  • The email address associated with your Emergency Ready account
  • Your full name (as registered)
  • Confirmation that you understand this action is irreversible
Verification: We may ask you to verify your identity by responding from your registered email address
