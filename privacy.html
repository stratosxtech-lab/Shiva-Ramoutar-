1. Introduction

StratosX Tech ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Emergency Ready mobile application (the "App" or "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, maintain, protect, and disclose information collected from users ("User," "you," or "your") of the Emergency Ready application.

By downloading, installing, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several categories of information to provide and improve our Service:

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and create a unique user identifier.
  • Profile Information: Name (optional), profile preferences, and notification settings.
  • Payment Information: When you subscribe to our premium service, payment information is collected and processed directly by Stripe, Inc. We do NOT store, process, or have access to your full credit card number, CVV, or banking details.

2.2 Location Information

  • Region Selection: We collect your selected country and region to provide localized weather alerts and emergency information.
  • Approximate Location: We may use IP-based geolocation to suggest your region. We do NOT collect precise GPS coordinates or track your real-time location.

2.3 Device Information

  • Device Identifiers: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers for push notifications.
  • App Usage Data: Features accessed, screens viewed, crash reports, and performance data.
  • Push Notification Tokens: Tokens required to deliver emergency alerts to your device.

2.4 User-Generated Content

  • Documents & Photos: Any documents, photos, or files you upload to the Document Storage feature are stored LOCALLY on your device only. We do NOT upload, access, or store this content on our servers.
  • Emergency Contacts: Contact information you add is stored locally on your device.
  • Checklist Data: Your emergency preparedness checklist progress is stored locally.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide weather alerts, emergency notifications, and app functionality.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, process subscriptions, and provide customer support.
  • Personalization: To customize content based on your selected region and preferences.
  • Communication: To send service-related announcements, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and improve app performance.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Stripe, Inc.: Payment processing (PCI DSS Level 1 certified). See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Expo/React Native: Push notification delivery infrastructure.
  • Cloud Hosting Providers: SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure providers for data storage.

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will provide notice before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3 (HTTPS).
  • Encryption at Rest: Database information is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit employee access to user data on a need-to-know basis.
  • Infrastructure Security: We use SOC 2 Type II compliant hosting providers with 24/7 monitoring.
  • Payment Security: All payment processing is handled by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: We retain your account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.
  • Transaction Records: Payment records are retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance purposes.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized usage analytics may be retained indefinitely for service improvement.
  • Deleted Accounts: Upon account deletion request, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
